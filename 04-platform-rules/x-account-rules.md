# X Account Rules

## Working Policy

The project treats current X platform rules as a hard boundary.

The Discrepancy Desk is an original fictional brand, but its government-adjacent lore and commentary/parody presentation create possible affiliation confusion. The project therefore adopts the conservative PCF posture documented in `06-research/x-policy-api-verification-2026-07-19.md`.

## Required Profile Posture

Use:

```text
Display name: Commentary | The Discrepancy Desk
Handle: @DiscrepancyDesk
PCF label: Commentary
```

The bio must clearly state that the persona is fictional/commentary and is not affiliated with any government agency.

Do not use:

- real agency seals or insignia;
- identical government branding;
- names or imagery that mimic a real official;
- claims of government employment;
- claims of classified access.

Recheck the official X Authenticity and Profile Labels policies before the public profile is finalized.

## Disallowed Behavior

The system must not:

- post identical or substantially similar content across many accounts;
- use multiple accounts to fake engagement;
- auto-reply;
- run keyword-triggered replies;
- auto-like;
- auto-follow;
- auto-repost;
- mass-DM;
- impersonate real agencies or people;
- obscure the account's fictional/commentary status.

## AI Use

Allowed direction:

```text
AI drafts → human reviews → human manually posts
```

Danger direction:

```text
AI scans keyword → AI replies automatically
```

Do not build the danger direction.

## First-Day Follow Strategy

Avoid following hundreds of accounts immediately.

A reasonable character-building first follow set is:

```text
1 archive/history/official-source account
1 bread baker / sourdough / old-recipe account
```

This creates human texture without fake-agency cosplay or coordinated amplification.
