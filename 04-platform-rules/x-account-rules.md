# X Account Rules

## Working Policy

The project treats X platform rules as a hard boundary.

Multiple accounts are allowed only if they are distinct and non-manipulative.

## Disallowed Behavior

The system must not:

- post identical/substantially similar content across many accounts
- use multiple accounts to fake engagement
- auto-reply
- keyword-trigger replies
- auto-like
- auto-follow
- auto-repost
- mass-DM
- impersonate real agencies or people

## AI Use

Allowed direction:

```text
AI drafts → human reviews → human manually posts
```

Danger direction:

```text
AI scans keyword → AI replies automatically
```

Do not build the danger direction.

## Profile Safety

Use “commentary/parody” and “fictional records custodian” framing.

Avoid real agency seals, logos, badges, and claims of classified access.

## First-Day Follow Strategy

Avoid following hundreds of accounts immediately.

A good character-building first follow set:

```text
1 archive/history/official source account
1 bread baker / sourdough / old recipe account
```

This creates human texture and avoids fake-agency cosplay.
