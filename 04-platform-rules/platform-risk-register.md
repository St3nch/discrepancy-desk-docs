# Platform Risk Register

## Risks

### Fake Official Appearance

Risk: account appears to impersonate government/intelligence agency.

Mitigation:

- use commentary/parody language
- fictional records custodian framing
- no real seals/logos/badges
- no real clearance claims

### Automation Risk

Risk: account gets flagged for spam/automation.

Mitigation:

- no auto-post/reply/like/follow
- manual posting first
- no mass follows on day one
- no duplicate content across accounts

### Conspiracy Certainty Risk

Risk: content crosses from fun weirdness into false certainty.

Mitigation:

- claim labels
- “pattern candidate” language
- avoid “confirmed/proves/undeniable”

### Multi-Account Coordination Risk

Risk: future accounts behave like fake amplification network.

Mitigation:

- distinct account purposes
- no cross-account auto-amplification
- no engagement pod features

### Source Quality Risk

Risk: recycled/hoax claims treated as original evidence.

Mitigation:

- source notes
- source independence score later
- Source Copying Detected label
