# Milestone 08 — Metrics Ledger and Editorial Learning

## Status
Not started.

## Goal
Turn owned-post observations into bounded, human-reviewed editorial learning without pretending unlike platform metrics are identical.

## Required Reading
- `LLM_MAP.md`
- `STATUS.md`
- `05-implementation-planning/implementation-roadmap.md`
- `02-product/product-overview.md`
- M04 and M07 completion records
- current platform metrics research

## Entry Gate
Enough owned posts and trustworthy snapshots exist to support analysis.

## Scope
Metric definitions, collection method labels, platform-specific fields, normalized comparison rules, content tags, weekly review questions, decision-driving versus vanity metrics, recommendation provenance.

## Out of Scope
Guaranteed growth claims, automated Truth Social polling, black-box scoring, autonomous strategy changes.

## Required Evidence
Metric dictionary; examples showing non-comparable metrics remain separate; review report using real owned-post data; tests for missing/partial snapshots.

## Exit Gate
The ledger explains what was observed, how it was measured, and what limited editorial inference is justified.

## Completion Record
Record date, evidence, accepted metric definitions, limitations, and next action.
