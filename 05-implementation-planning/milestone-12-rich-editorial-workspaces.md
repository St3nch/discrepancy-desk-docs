## Goal
Expand the proven desktop product into richer growth and publishing workflows without weakening approval or evidence contracts.

## Entry Gate
Tauri in stable daily use; core publication contracts proven; each subpackage separately admitted based on operator demand.

## Admitted Subpackages
- Reply Desk: contextual candidate replies, exact approval, manual posting, reconciliation, metrics
- Article Room: thesis, outline, research packet, sections, citations, dependent launch/excerpt posts
- Asset Library: rights, dimensions, variants, crops, alt text, reuse history; evidence remains immutable
- advanced Calendar/Pipeline: drag/drop, multi-select, heatmaps, swimlanes, aging, richer saved views, keyboard-first operation
- richer search and native operator productivity

## Out of Scope
Autonomous engagement, approval bypass, assets overwriting evidence, hidden dependency mutation, features without demonstrated operator need.

## Exit Gate
Each admitted subpackage proves exact revision approval, dependency/correction history, manual external action, accurate reconciliation, asset/evidence separation, and no hidden authority.
