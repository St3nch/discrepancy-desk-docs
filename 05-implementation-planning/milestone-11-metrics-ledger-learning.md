## Goal
Turn trustworthy owned-post observations into human-reviewed editorial learning without making the database or LLM an autonomous strategy engine.

## Entry Gate
Sufficient real history; metrics vocabulary and current monetization rules reverified; platform-equivalence and hypothesis/experiment boundaries approved.

## Scope
- post/topic/format/time/series observations
- rolling 90-day monetization progress with dated configurable criteria
- explicit missing/unavailable states
- observation, correlation, hypothesis, experiment, accepted-conclusion separation
- human-reviewed experiments and follow-up prompts

## Out of Scope
Autonomous schedule optimization, automatic strategy, performance-driven approval/deletion, weak filler, unsupported causation, false platform equivalence.

## Exit Gate
Metrics preserve observation truth; rolling windows and missing states are correct; every recommendation cites observations and labels uncertainty; experiments require human acceptance; changing policy does not rewrite historical meaning.
