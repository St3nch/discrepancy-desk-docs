## Status
Not started.

## Goal
Reduce capture friction without creating a scraper or background collector.

## Entry Gate
MVP workflow stable; capture pain is measured; platform policy boundaries are current.

## Scope
Human click per item, local-only handoff, visible confirmation/edit, parser failure fallback, provenance, X first.

## Out of Scope
Background scripts, timeline/profile crawling, bulk capture, session-cookie reuse, undocumented APIs, automated screenshots, automated publishing.

## Truth Social Gate
Truth Social extraction is disabled unless later research or written permission explicitly admits it. URL-and-human-note support may remain manual.

## Required Evidence
Permission matrix, threat review, manual tests, parser-failure tests, volume-drift safeguards, proof no background collection occurs.

## Exit Gate
An admitted X capture helper is useful and fail-closed, or the project records a justified decision to retain manual URL/note capture.

## Completion Record
Record date, evidence, tests, platform admissions, deviations, and next action.
