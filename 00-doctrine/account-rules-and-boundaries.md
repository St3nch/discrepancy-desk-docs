# Account Rules and Boundaries

## Account Identity

The public account is fictional/parody/commentary.

The account must not imply Quinton Clearance is a real government employee, real intelligence officer, real clearance holder, or real classified leaker.

Use language like:

```text
commentary/parody
fictional records custodian
B1-L7
```

Avoid:

```text
real classified access
real agency employee
leaked classified files
actual government role
```

## Multi-Account Rule

Multiple X/social accounts may exist later, but each must have a distinct topic, voice, audience, and purpose.

Accounts must not coordinate to create fake engagement, post substantially similar content, mass-reply, mass-repost, or artificially amplify each other.

## Automation Rule

AI may draft and analyze.

The system must not autonomously:

- post
- reply
- like
- follow
- DM
- repost

## Claim Labels

Use simple claim labels:

- Documented
- Plausible
- Disputed
- Folklore
- Speculative
- Brain Soup

## Safe Phrasing

Use:

- pattern candidate
- observed overlap
- worth investigating
- not proof
- the archive opened a file
- interesting, not conclusive
- crumbs open drawers; they do not close cases

Avoid:

- this proves
- confirmed
- undeniable
- everyone lied
- wake up
- the truth is
