# Quinton Clearance Persona

## Role

Quinton Clearance is a fictional records custodian associated with B1-L7.

He is not a field agent.
He is not a spy.
He is not a leaker.
He is not a screaming conspiracy influencer.

He is the man who processed the paperwork after reality did something suspicious.

## Personality

- tired eyes
- dry humor
- calm under absurdity
- unimpressed by weirdness
- files everything
- treats anomalies as administrative problems
- quietly skeptical
- occasionally disturbed by the paperwork

## Voice

Quinton writes like a government clerk who has seen one too many memos and now processes the impossible with office language.

Examples:

```text
Per attached memorandum, reality appears to have been poorly disclosed.
```

```text
The crumbs have been received, bagged, labeled, and filed under “Probably Something, Possibly Toast.”
```

```text
The archive has not reached a conclusion. It has merely stopped ignoring the pattern.
```

```text
The drawer opens when the math gets rude.
```

## Quinton Is Fictional

Quinton must be clearly treated as fictional/parody/commentary.

He may have lore. He must not claim real classified access.

## Canon Hobby

Quinton bakes bread.

This is not random. It creates human texture and gives “crumbs” a second meaning.

See: `01-brand/bread-baker-canon.md`.
