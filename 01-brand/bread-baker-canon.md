# Bread Baker Canon

## Decision

Quinton Clearance has a strangely normal hobby: bread baking.

Specifically:

```text
sourdough
old recipes
Depression-era recipes
Victorian baking
old government cafeteria recipes
coffee cake
suspiciously serious loaves
```

## Why It Works

- “Crumbs” double meaning.
- Humanizes the account.
- Contrasts with weird files.
- Fits slow, patient, procedural clerk energy.
- Creates running jokes that do not derail the core topic.
