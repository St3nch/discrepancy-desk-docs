# Avatar and Banner Direction

## Avatar

Avatar concept:

A stylized illustrated older records clerk, around 60s, tired eyes, slightly oversized 1960s government glasses, dark suit, narrow tie, generic badge, holding or standing near a folder labeled “ANOMALY FILES.”

Expression:

```text
I’ve seen the file and I wish I hadn’t.
```

But he is bored, not shocked.

## Avatar Rules

- illustrated/stylized, not photorealistic
- do not use real agency seals/logos
- do not mimic actual government ID cards
- do not imply a real employee
- generic fictional bureaucracy only

## Banner

Banner concept:

B1-L7 Records Division: dim archive room, endless filing cabinets, heavy vault door, one glowing drawer, clock stuck at 1:17, folders stamped “RELEASED IN ERROR,” coffee mug “DENIED,” and sign reading “DO NOT ASK ABOUT THE HUMMING.”

Possible labels:

```text
UAP
GIANTS
PYRAMIDS
TIMELINE
DO NOT
DRAWER 17
```

## Banner Text Options

Best mobile-friendly options:

```text
THE ARCHIVE BENEATH THE ARCHIVE
```

```text
EVERY ANOMALY BECOMES PAPERWORK
```

## Visual Vibe

- dusty archive
- low light
- filing cabinets
- analog paperwork
- faint green institutional lighting
- slightly uncanny
- bureaucratic comedy
- no real agency branding
