## Approved Quinton Clearance Avatar

The approved avatar is a square, stylized editorial-cartoon portrait designed to remain readable inside X's circular profile crop and to support future character reuse in thumbnails, video, or animation.

Quinton is depicted as:

- approximately 55 years old
- short, with compact proportions
- lean-to-average rather than heavy
- narrow, slightly weathered face
- short, messy gray hair
- rectangular glasses
- tired eyes and expressive eyebrows
- dry, mildly skeptical expression
- pale dress shirt
- loosened dark tie
- worn brown-gray cardigan

The image uses clean, animation-friendly linework, restrained caricature, muted institutional colors, and a softly blurred basement-records background. The glasses, eyebrows, face shape, cardigan, tie, and skeptical expression are the current canonical visual anchors.

## Avatar Rules

- illustrated and stylized, not photorealistic
- readable at small social-avatar size
- face remains the dominant element
- no real agency seal, logo, badge, or government identification
- no weapons
- no text required in the avatar
- do not imply Quinton is a real government employee
- future variants should preserve the approved character identity unless the owner approves a redesign

## Approved X Banner

The approved banner is a wide cinematic editorial-cartoon scene set in the B1-L7 office.

The foreground is a dusty but functional modern basement records office with stale institutional lighting. A large circular vault door stands open. Quinton is shown from the back or side, with no visible face, calmly walking through the vault while carrying a file. His compact silhouette, cardigan, and routine body language connect him to the avatar without duplicating the portrait.

Beyond the vault is an unexpectedly clean, blue-white, high-technology records environment with server-like archive columns, illuminated panels, and a deeper corridor. The visual joke is the contrast:

```text
dusty paperwork bureaucracy outside
impossibly advanced records infrastructure inside
```

Supporting details include:

- a single framed portrait of President Donald Trump
- a respectfully displayed American flag on a standing pole
- a restrained corkboard and office wall
- filing cabinets, binders, folders, desk lamp, clock, and archive materials
- bread-baking book and subtle conspiracy curiosities on the desk
- a visually quiet lower-left area for X's overlapping profile picture

Background lettering is not intended to be read at profile size. It functions as visual office and paperwork texture.

## Banner Rules

- preserve the 3:1 X banner composition
- keep the avatar-overlap area visually quiet
- maintain the contrast between the stale office and high-tech vault interior
- Quinton's face should not be visible in this banner
- the American flag must remain respectfully displayed
- no real agency seals or official insignia
- no weapons
- avoid excessive wall clutter
- future banners may expand the world but should remain visually consistent with the approved avatar
