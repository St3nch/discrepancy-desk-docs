## Lore Location

**Basement 1, Level 7** / **B1-L7**

B1-L7 contains subtle “Q” numerology:

- B1 = Basement 1
- L7 = Level 7
- 17th letter = Q

## Brand Definition

The Discrepancy Desk is the fictional records office where strange overlaps, hidden histories, folklore, anomalies, conspiracy lore, and poorly disclosed reality are logged as paperwork.

## Core Brand Lines

> Every anomaly becomes paperwork.

> Per attached memorandum, reality appears to have been poorly disclosed.

> The archive beneath the archive.

> No anomaly leaves B1-L7 without clearance.

> The red string is not evidence. It is a filing request.

> Crumbs open drawers. They do not close cases.

## Tone

- deadpan
- bureaucratic
- weird
- dry-funny
- calm
- skeptical
- evidence-aware
- under-reacts to absurd material

## Audience Promise

The account opens strange files, labels what is known/unknown, and makes the rabbit hole funny without becoming a panic cult.
