# Naming Research

## Names Considered

- Anomaly Archive
- Archive Level 7
- B1L7
- Quinton Clearance
- Redacted Archive
- Discrepancy Desk
- The Discrepancy Desk
- Drawer 17
- Filed Under Weird
- The Unfiled Archive
- Redacted Records
- Blackfile Bureau
- Forbidden Footnotes

## Decision

Public brand selected:

```text
The Discrepancy Desk
```

Handle/domain form:

```text
DiscrepancyDesk
```

## Why This Won

- available across important socials
- exact `.com` available and purchased
- readable
- unique enough
- perfect bureaucratic weirdness
- fits Quinton Clearance
- not as generic/crowded as Anomaly Archive or Redacted Archive

## Rejected / Not Primary

### Anomaly Archive

Good internal concept, but too generic/crowded for public brand.

### Redacted Archive

Strong but taken/crowded.

### B1L7

Cool but too cryptic as primary public brand. Still useful as lore.

### Quinton Clearance

Good persona-first handle, but The Discrepancy Desk is stronger as the public institution.
